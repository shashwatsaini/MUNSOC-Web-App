COPE Edition I



22nd - 23rd March 2024

The COPE Edition I embodies our cherished ideals. Through formal debate, our society provides an unimpeded avenue for diverse ideologies, fostering a platform where opinions soar freely. Here, bias takes a backseat, and merit alone prevails. This inaugural event welcomes participants across three committees, engaging in discussions on highly politicized and pressing agendas.





About Us



Established in 2023, the Model United Nations Society at Dayananda Sagar University is committed to raising awareness about global events and influential personalities. Our mission centers on cultivating indispensable skills—diplomacy, leadership, research, and eloquence—crucial in navigating today's complex world.

In an era fraught with challenges, it is imperative to develop a perspective that accommodates all parties. Attacks on free speech, relics of a bygone era, must be consigned to history. As torchbearers of the next generation, we bear the responsibility of safeguarding principles bestowed upon us by democratic institutions, a free press, and international cooperation.




About Dayananda Sagar University



In the six decades since the establishment of Dayananda Sagar Institutions in 1960, the group has grown into one of India’s leading educational institutions today. This growth is the result of the institutions focused effort to advance knowledge and educate students in all disciplines, from medicine to engineering, and law to commerce. The pursuit of excellence, fairness, leadership, and integrity & transparency are values that drive our institutions.
